## 📊 What is OpenFolio?

OpenFolio is a personal finance dashboard that lets you track all your investments in one place — stocks, mutual funds, commodities and more. Built for investors who want clarity, not complexity.

Licensed under **GNU GPL v3.0** — anyone who modifies and deploys OpenFolio as a public service must also open source their changes.
